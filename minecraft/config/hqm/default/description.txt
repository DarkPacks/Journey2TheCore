Welcome to Journey 2 the Core, a hardcore Minecraft modpack inspired by Dante's Inferno and Journey to the Center of the Earth. In this treacherous and challenging adventure, you will brave the depths of a 750 block tall world, braving dangers and solving puzzles as you journey to the very core of the earth. From perilous caves and lava-filled dungeons, to the frozen tundras and scorching deserts, you will face a multitude of challenges and obstacles on your quest.

But fear not, for with determination and cunning, you will overcome all obstacles and emerge victorious. Are you ready to embark on this epic journey? Gather your gear and prepare for the ultimate test of endurance and skill. The journey to the core awaits!
